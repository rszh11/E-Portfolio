Entry #4

Story – A Big Change

📋

Moving from nursery to primary school was a big event for me. Before the school year started, I had so many memories and had been playing with the same friends every day.


Unfortunately, I had to go to a different school than them. On my first day of 1st class, while I was walking into the new classroom, I felt very scared, but I saw most of the others were also nervous — so it calmed me down. Sadly, my old friends had gone to another school.


However, I sat next to a nice boy, and we became friends quickly. This event still makes me think about my childhood. It taught me that big changes can be scary, but you can always learn something from them.
