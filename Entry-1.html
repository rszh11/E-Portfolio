Entry #1

Summer Updates

Three highlights from my summer, plus one lowlight — and how I dealt with it.

🇧🇪 Belgium 🇭🇺 Budapest 🇳🇱 Netherlands

✦ Three Highlights

Highlight 1
Belgium
We went to the flea market, bought some stuff and enjoyed the view there. The food was really tasty, but it was difficult to find a good restaurant where 8 people could sit together.
Highlight 2
Budapest
We also went to Széchenyi baths and pool, which was nice — it's really pretty and the place was relaxing since it wasn't crowded. We also visited some memorable places in Budapest, which looked beautiful at night.
Highlight 3
Netherlands
The architecture was incredibly designed. I also liked the food — for example the honey waffles you could buy in every store. I was shocked when I saw the parking prices there; they're doing this to make driving less attractive.

✦ One Lowlight

Lowlight

Travelling

The lowlight of that summer holiday was that, while travelling by car, we got a scratch on our rental van because we had to park in a parking building. But I'd say we learned something from that accident: never stress about a parking spot that goes down.

💡
My Solution

Our solution was that we had insurance and just had to pay CHF 500. Next time we'll check the hotel parking before we drive in.
