Entry #3

Airfare Interview

I interviewed myself using the questions from page 27. Here are the questions and my answers.

About Me

My name is Rishi and I'm the writer of this e-portfolio. I'm doing my ICT apprenticeship now, so my teacher Mr. Heeg thought we could do this year's entry as a website.

Part 1: Flying Experiences

How often do you fly? What kinds of airlines do you normally use?
I fly about once or twice a year. I usually use airlines which are cheap for the trip and highly reliable.
When was the last flight you took? Where did you go? What for? Where did you sit?
My last flight was last spring break. I went to Egypt and Türkiye for holidays with my family — we planned the trip just 3 days before. I sat in a window seat, which I got randomly on every flight.
Have you ever flown long-haul? Where did you go? How long was the flight? Did you get jet lag?
Yes, I flew to New York three years ago. The flight took about 8 hours. I never got jet lag because I could adapt to the new time easily.
How do you feel about flying? Have you ever had a very bad experience on a flight?
I like flying because it's fast. I've never had a really bad experience, just some normal turbulence.

Part 2: "Have you ever..." Questions

Have you ever been very delayed when travelling? How long for?
Yes, my flight from Türkiye was delayed by half an hour. I had to wait at the airport gate a little longer than usual.
Have you ever missed a flight, train, or bus? Why? What did you do?
I missed my bus for work because I left home too late, but I still caught my usual train.
Have you ever had to sit near a screaming baby on a plane, train, or bus? What did you do?
Yes, on a train — some kids were playing for hours. I just put on my headphones and listened to music.
Had to catch a connection flight, train, or bus with very little time to spare? Did you catch it?
I had a crazy connection flight in India — we almost missed it, so they treated us like VIPs and let us pass through security and other procedures quickly. And guess what? We caught it! I also take a daily train or bus, which is often really hard to catch, but I've been lucky so far and haven't missed one yet.
My Reflection

This interview was a little bit weird, because I had to interview myself. But it refreshed my memories.
