Future Predictions

For this entry, I'm making predictions about the future using the future continuous and future perfect tenses — here's how I think our world will look in a few decades.

🛍️

Social Media & Shopping

In 2050, we will be using AI assistants to do all our online shopping for us. By that time, online shops like now will have disappeared completely because apps will know exactly what we want to buy before we even ask — probably because of the algorithm.

🏥

Health, Medicine & Housing

Smart tech will be monitoring our health from a phone 24/7. Robots will have built modern, air-conditioned houses for everyone, and intelligent systems will fix any technical problems automatically.

🗳️

Fashion & Politics

People will be wearing high-tech clothes that change color based on our mood. In politics, digital voting systems will have replaced voting on paper everywhere, making decisions much faster and more secure — like Swiyu already being integrated with the E-ID.

My Reflection

As an ICT student, it's exciting to think about the future. Most of these changes could be built on new software and smart technology.
