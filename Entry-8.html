Murder Mystery

Murder in Paradise — a logic game where I played detective on a tropical island to find out who killed millionaire Joel Arnheim.

The Mystery Plan

1. The Plot

The crime happened on Paradise Island, where rich businessman Joel Arnheim was murdered in his room. The police had four main suspects: his wife Gillian, the cook Pedro and his business partners Nigel and Alberto.

👩
Gillian
Said she left her husband alone because she felt unwell.
👨‍🍳
Pedro
Said he was busy working in the kitchen all night.
🥃
Nigel
Was drunk and pretended he never left the lounge.
🕴️
Alberto
Also said he stayed in the lounge all evening.

2. My Role & Strategy

I took on the role of the detective. To get ready for the game, I studied the case facts and memorized the details so I could interview the suspects and catch them in any lies.

3. The Investigation

During the game, I questioned everyone one by one. I spent around five minutes with each suspect, asking about their connection to Joel, their stories, and where exactly they were when the murder happened.

4. My Theory

My main suspect was Gillian. It seemed highly suspicious that she walked to a completely different restroom and disappeared for 30 minutes. In the end, though, my theory was incorrect.

5. The Twist

The final reveal completely shocked me! The murder was actually a team effort by Alberto, Pedro and Gillian. It turned out Alberto and Gillian were secretly dating and Alberto gave Pedro 200,000 CHF to get rid of Joel. I never saw that coming!
